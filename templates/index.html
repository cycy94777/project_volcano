The Global Volcano project,
a product of the Smithsonian Institution, dedicates itself to “document, disseminate, and understand” volcanic activity around the world. Over the years, they have compiled and maintained a list of all documented activities within a database. The dataset contains over 1300 volcanoes with properties that include geographical location, primary volcano type, last known eruption time, elevation, dominant rock type, and tectonic setting.
